my grasp of harmony

I love music...

...but I used to feel completely lost with music theory!

The theory of musical harmony can feel like an unnatural, overcomplicated way to describe what our ears, on the other hand, understand intuitively. Yet its principles and most of its concepts are actually very relatable, and they can be grasped progressively, relying on our perception of music.

This site is an invitation to follow my journey through that understanding: a step-by-step exploration of harmony, where we will encounter music theory concepts, always guided by intuition.
