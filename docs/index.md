# my grasp of harmony

!!! note "I love music..."
    ...but I used to feel really lost with music theory!


The theory of musical harmony can feel like an unnatural, overcomplicated way to describe what our ears, on the other hand, understand _intuitively_. Yet its principles and most of its concepts are actually very relatable, and they can be grasped progressively, relying on our perception of music.

This site is an invitation to follow my journey through that understanding: a step-by-step exploration of harmony, where we will encounter music theory concepts, always guided by intuition.
